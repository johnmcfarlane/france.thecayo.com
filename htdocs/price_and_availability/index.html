Notes

There may be enquiries for other periods in the pipeline.

There is a discount for more than two weeks (up to £25 per extra week, depending on the time of year). The weeks always run Saturday to Saturday (4pm start and 10am end).

** New 2005 onwards - Prices include Final Clean!**

The price includes consumable services (water and electricity) at a nominal rate of £per week. There is also a telephone for incoming calls; outgoing calls can be made using a phonecard (eg BT) though local calls can be made free of charge (eg for booking restaurants, checking local events, shop opening times, etc.)
