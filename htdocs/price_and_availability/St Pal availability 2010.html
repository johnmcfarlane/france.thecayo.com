There is a discount for more than two weeks (up to £25 per extra week, depending on the time of year). The weeks always run Saturday to Saturday (4pm start and 10am end).

** New 2005 onwards - Prices include Final Clean!** 

The price includes consumable services (water and electricity) at a nominal rate of £20 per week. There is also a telephone for incoming calls; outgoing calls can be made using a phonecard (eg BT) though local calls can be made free of charge (eg for booking restaurants, checking local events, shop opening times, etc.)  Linen is avalable from the Auberge next door at a nominal charge.
