On Departure

Revision 25.02.06

If other tenants are expected within the next 24 hours you may leave the electricity and water turned on. Otherwise, please turn off the electricity at the fuse box as well as the two fuse switches for hot water and heating.The water should be turned off at the base of the hot water tank and at the toilet cistern. Unless you know that there are other visitors within a few days, please also turn off the stopcock in the water cupboard downstairs.

Please leave fridge defrosted well before you leave.

In case of bad weather and/or if no other visitors are due, please bring in from the patio the hardwood parasol (the base can be left outside) and plastic patio furniture and close all windows and shutters, especially the Terrace Awning.

In cold damp wintry weather, condensation can be a problem if the apartment has been left empty for some time. The building has a ventilation system that works best if all the internal doors are left open (ie bedroom, bathroom, cupboard and WC).
