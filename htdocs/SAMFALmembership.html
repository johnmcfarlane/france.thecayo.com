Company No. 6045487        Registered Charity No. 1126502

Membership of the  Association is open to any member of the community who is no less than 18 years old.  Younger people can join as part of a Family Membership.  There is a small annual subscription (see below).

Associations or Corporate Bodies (eg local firms) can also take out membership.  There is a higher rate of subscription for commercial organisations.

More detailed information can be found  in paragraphs 4 to 12 of the Articles of Association, which form part of the Governing ocument of the Association.  This can be downloaded as a Word file from the web site.

The Trustees have the final say on admission to membersip; this power has been adopted as  a safeguard to prevent the Assciation being taken over by a special interest group, who may wish the field to be used for other purposes (eg as building land).
